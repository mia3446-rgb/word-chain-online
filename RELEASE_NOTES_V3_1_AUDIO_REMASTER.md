# WORD CHAIN ARENA V3.1 — Audio Remaster

- Replaced flat sustained-tone BGM with original rhythmic chord, bass, and melody loops for lobby, normal, and ranked play.
- Added smooth context switching and fade behavior while preserving mobile audio unlock and saved volume settings.
- Correct-word audio now plays one musical hit per Korean character.
- Longer words accelerate into a rapid arcade-style burst.
- GREAT, AMAZING, and LEGENDARY words add richer chord, sweep, noise, and sparkle layers.
- Reworked click, modal, purchase, warning, victory, defeat, promotion, demotion, turn, and match-found effects.
- No copyrighted audio files are included; all audio is generated at runtime with Web Audio API.
